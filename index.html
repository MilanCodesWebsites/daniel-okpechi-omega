What services do you offer?

I specialize in web design, branding, UI/UX, and Figma/Framer development, creating modern, user-friendly experiences tailored to your needs.

How do I start working with you?

Simply reach out through the contact form or email. We'll schedule an initial consultation to discuss your project requirements, timeline, and budget before proceeding.

What design tools do you use?

I primarily work with Figma for UI/UX design, Adobe Creative Suite for graphics, and Framer for interactive prototypes. I also use various specialized tools depending on project requirements.

Do you offer development services?

Yes, I offer front-end development services and can collaborate with back-end developers for full-stack projects. My development expertise includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React.

What is your pricing structure?

I offer both project-based and hourly pricing options. Project-based pricing is determined by scope, complexity, and timeline. For ongoing work, I provide retainer packages with discounted rates. I'll provide a detailed quote after our initial consultation.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Website redesigns are a core service I offer. I'll analyze your current site, identify improvement areas, and create a modern, user-friendly design that aligns with your brand and business goals.
