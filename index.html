I'm Daniel Okpechi, a multidisciplinary Web Designer & Developer based in the heart of Lagos. I specialize in creating intuitive digital experiences that align with your business goals.


With a strong foundation in both design thinking and frontend development, I bridge the gap between creative ideas and functional, responsive code. Whether it’s designing clean user interfaces or bringing them to life through smooth, pixel-perfect layouts — I focus on delivering work that combines visual appeal with real-world usability.


When it comes to UI/UX design, I’m equal parts artist, problem-solver, and occasional pixel-perfectionist. I believe great design isn’t just about looking good — it’s about making people’s lives easier (and maybe even making them smile while they’re at it). Whether I’m mapping out user flows, building wireframes, or debating the emotional impact of a button's hover state, I bring empathy, curiosity, and just the right amount of caffeine to every project. If the user doesn’t notice the design, I take it as a win — because that means it’s doing exactly what it should.

My Tools and Stack

These are the professional tools I use to design, prototype, and manage my projects efficiently.

Figma

I use Figma to collaborate with my team on UI/UX designs in real-time. It's my go-to tool for creating wireframes, interactive prototypes, and design systems that ensure consistency across projects.

Adobe XD

I use Adobe XD to create detailed user interfaces and interactive prototypes. Its integration with other Adobe products helps me streamline my workflow when working on complex design projects.

Whimsical

I use Whimsical to quickly visualize ideas and create flowcharts for user journeys. It helps me communicate complex processes to stakeholders and team members in an intuitive, visual format.

Miro

I use Miro as my digital whiteboard for brainstorming sessions and remote workshops. It's essential for my collaborative planning process, especially when working with distributed teams across different time zones.

ClickUp

I use ClickUp to manage all my project tasks and timelines. It helps me stay organized with its customizable workflows, allowing me to track progress and meet deadlines consistently across multiple projects.

Zeplin

I use Zeplin to bridge the gap between design and development. It allows me to share design specs, assets, and code snippets with developers, ensuring my designs are implemented exactly as intended.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and pricing. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us directly.

What services do you offer?

I specialize in web design, branding, UI/UX, and Figma/Framer development, creating modern, user-friendly experiences tailored to your needs.

How do I start working with you?

Simply reach out through the contact form or email. We'll schedule an initial consultation to discuss your project requirements, timeline, and budget before proceeding.

What design tools do you use?

I primarily work with Figma for UI/UX design, Adobe Creative Suite for graphics, and Framer for interactive prototypes. I also use various specialized tools depending on project requirements.

Do you offer development services?

Yes, I offer front-end development services and can collaborate with back-end developers for full-stack projects. My development expertise includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React.

What is your pricing structure?

I offer both project-based and hourly pricing options. Project-based pricing is determined by scope, complexity, and timeline. For ongoing work, I provide retainer packages with discounted rates. I'll provide a detailed quote after our initial consultation.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Website redesigns are a core service I offer. I'll analyze your current site, identify improvement areas, and create a modern, user-friendly design that aligns with your brand and business goals.
