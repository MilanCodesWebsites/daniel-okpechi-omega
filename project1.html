Apple Vision Pro

Designed a visually immersive, responsive landing page for the Apple Vision Pro. The aim was to reflect Apple’s futuristic hardware through a clean, minimal interface with bold visuals and intuitive structure—crafted for both mobile and desktop viewing.

Project Goal

The goal was to create a product-first experience that immediately communicates the power and uniqueness of spatial computing. The landing page needed to be bold, lightweight, and scalable—capturing attention while clearly explaining benefits.

The Challenge

One major challenge was conveying the immersive nature of the device through 2D UI. I solved this by leveraging large hero visuals, layered motion elements, and scroll-triggered interactions while keeping page performance optimal.

The Result

The final design delivered a clean, engaging landing page experience that emphasized innovation and clarity. It improved conversion rates, received positive user feedback, and demonstrated best practices in responsive UX/UI design.
