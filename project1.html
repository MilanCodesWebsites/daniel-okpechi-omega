Apple Vision Pro – Landing Page Design

I designed a landing page for the Apple Vision Pro that showcases the device’s innovation through sleek UI and immersive visuals. The goal was to mirror Apple’s minimalism while introducing a bold, responsive experience.

Goal

Create a product-focused page that quickly communicates the Vision Pro’s spatial computing power and benefits. The design needed to feel both premium and approachable.

Challenge

Translating a 3D immersive product into a 2D interface was the toughest part. I used motion layers, interactive scroll sections, and clean layouts to simulate depth and engagement.

Result

The final product is a fast, visually-rich landing page that aligns with Apple’s branding and improves user engagement. It’s optimized for both performance and aesthetic clarity.
